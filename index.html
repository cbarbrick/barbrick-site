FAQ
Common Questions
Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What types of startups do you work with?

+

I work with startups and scale-ups across industries — SaaS, fintech, healthtech, e-commerce, and more. If you have data and a business problem that AI could solve, we should talk.

Do I need a data team already?

+

No. I work with teams of all sizes. For early-stage startups without a data team, I can handle strategy through implementation. For teams with existing engineers, I complement what you already have.

How long does a typical project take?

+

An AI Readiness Assessment takes about 2 weeks. Full build projects typically run 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. I prioritize speed without cutting corners.

What if I'm not sure AI is right for my business?

+

That's exactly what the strategy call is for. I'll be honest with you — if AI isn't the right move yet, I'll tell you. The Readiness Assessment is designed to answer this question definitively.

Can I start with the assessment and upgrade later?

+

Absolutely. Most clients start with the AI Readiness Assessment, and about 80% move forward with a build engagement. The assessment fee is credited toward larger projects.
